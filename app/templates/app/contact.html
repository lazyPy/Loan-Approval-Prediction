Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for a vehicle loan, you'll need to provide the following documents:

  • Valid government-issued ID (e.g., passport, driver's license)
  • Proof of income (e.g., pay slips, income tax returns)
  • Proof of address (e.g., utility bills)
  • Vehicle details and quotation from the dealer

Additional documents may be required depending on your employment status and loan amount.

Our loan approval process typically takes 3-5 business days after all required documents have been submitted. The process includes:

  1. Initial document verification (1 day)
  2. Credit investigation (1-2 days)
  3. Loan approval (1-2 days)

Once approved, the loan disbursement usually occurs within 24-48 hours.

Our current interest rates start from 10% per annum. We offer flexible loan terms ranging from 12 to 36 months.

The exact interest rate applicable to your loan will depend on factors such as:

  • Loan amount
  • Loan term
  • Your credit history
  • Down payment percentage

For a personalized quote, please use our Loan Calculator or contact our loan officers.

You can check the status of your loan application in several ways:

  1. Visit our Check Status page and enter your reference number.
  2. Call our customer support at (+63) 917-123-4567.
  3. Email us at support@financingsolutions.ph with your reference number in the subject line.
  4. Visit our office and speak with a loan officer.

Please keep your reference number handy as it will be required to retrieve your application status.
